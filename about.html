What To Expect At ONE80

The students can expect a talk from the Bible (that's not boring) focused on the things that teens are going through, intentional games, opportunities to connect and ask questions, plus time to just hang out and get to know each other

Middle School: (grades 6-8) meets from 6:30-8:00 on Wednesday nights at the church.

High School: (grades 9-12) meets from 6:30-8:00 on Sunday nights at the church.
